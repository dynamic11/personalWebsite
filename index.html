Hello World!

I am in the last semester of my Masters in Electrical Engineering. The focus of my studies has been Computer-Aided Design for the simulation of electrical circuits. I enjoy my area of research since it exposed me to the application of mathematics/numerical methods to efficiently simulate complex electrical circuits.

Outside of my area of research, I enjoy computer programming and web application design. I try to take every opportunity to learn new technologies and to develop helpful tools. I also try to put my skills to good use by making apps and websites to help out small businesses.

Recently, I have been investing my free time to get a better understanding of the topic of Machine Learning. During my masters, I took a course on the application of Machine Learning for the simulation of electrical circuits. This gave me a more academic perspective on the topic. It is an interesting yet complex area that I am very interested to explore with strong guidance.
